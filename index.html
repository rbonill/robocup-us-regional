What is RoboCup?

The Robot World Cup Initiative (RoboCup) is a vehicle to promote robotics and AI research by offering a publicly appealing, but formidable challenge. The proposed task for the robot soccer domain is: By mid-21st century, a team of fully autonomous humanoid robot soccer players shall win a soccer game, complying with the official rules of FIFA, against the winner of the most recent World Cup.

In order to enter a team in the RoboCup competition, researchers must integrate technologies in many different areas. Therefore, RoboCup is both a landmark project and a standard problem for AI and robotics researchers to use in evaluating their work.

What is the aim of the RoboCup US Regional Committee?

The committee promotes the goals of the International RoboCup Federation in the United States. It aims to advance the RoboCup initiative in the US by organizing local events and connecting research groups that share common research interests.

Contact us at us-regcommittee@robocup.org to learn more, register your team, or connect with other US-based participants.
